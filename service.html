Details of our services

Skinny Cat: Weight Loss Program

Description

Designed for individuals aiming to shed extra pounds, the Skinny Cat program focuses on high-intensity workouts and a balanced diet.

Details

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Frequency: 4 times a week
  • Sessions: Cardio, strength training, and HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)
  • Diet Plan: Personalized meal plans
  • Support: Weekly progress check-ins with a personal trainer

Fat Cat: Bulk Up Program

Description

Aimed at those looking to gain muscle mass, the Fat Cat program combines weightlifting with a high-protein diet.

Details

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Frequency: 5 times a week
  • Sessions: Weightlifting, resistance training, and hypertrophy-focused workouts
  • Diet Plan: High-protein meal plans
  • Support: Bi-weekly consultations with a nutritionist

Agile Cat: Flexibility and Mobility Program

Description

Perfect for improving flexibility, balance, and overall mobility, the Agile Cat program integrates yoga, Pilates, and stretching exercises.

Details

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Frequency: 3 times a week
  • Sessions: Yoga, Pilates, and dynamic stretching
  • Diet Plan: Anti-inflammatory meal suggestions
  • Support: Monthly progress assessments with a flexibility coach

Strong Cat: Strength and Conditioning Program

Description

For those wanting to build overall strength and endurance, the Strong Cat program features varied workouts to enhance physical conditioning.

Details

  • Duration: 10 weeks
  • Frequency: 4 times a week
  • Sessions: Circuit training, functional exercises, and core strengthening
  • Diet Plan: Balanced diet with emphasis on protein and complex carbs
  • Support: Weekly group training sessions

Fit Cat: General Fitness Program

Description

The Fit Cat program is designed for overall fitness improvement, suitable for all fitness levels, combining various types of exercises.

Details

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Frequency: 3 times a week
  • Sessions: Mix of cardio, strength training, and flexibility exercises
  • Diet Plan: General healthy eating guidelines
  • Support: Monthly fitness evaluations

Zen Cat: Mind-Body Wellness Program

Description

Focused on holistic well-being, the Zen Cat program incorporates mindfulness practices along with physical exercises to reduce stress and improve mental health.

Details

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Frequency: 2 times a week
  • Sessions: Meditation, light yoga, and breathwork
  • Diet Plan: Whole foods diet with mindfulness eating practices
  • Support: Bi-weekly group meditation sessions

Power Cat: High-Performance Training Program

Description

Geared towards athletes and fitness enthusiasts, the Power Cat program aims to enhance performance through specialized training.

Details

  • Duration: 10 weeks
  • Frequency: 5 times a week
  • Sessions: Plyometrics, agility drills, and sport-specific training
  • Diet Plan: High-energy meal plans with supplements
  • Support: Access to sports physiotherapist
